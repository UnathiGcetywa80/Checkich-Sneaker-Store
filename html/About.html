About Us

A Value Carried From Generation To Generation Chekich which reached to all corners of Turkey with its quality and original designs, entered the sector in 2014. Today, it has been positioned as a leading company in the sector as a brand that cooperates with the world's biggest brands and makes a difference with its line. Chekich, having the mission of quality in shoes with 100% Vegan Leather handwork; over time, succeeded original and quality designs in its products. Meanwhile, Chekich produces 90% of its products in Turkey, also devotes 10% of place in its store for the design of the world-famous Italian, French and Spanish brands which it is in cooperation with.

100% Vegan Products We guarantee that we do not use any animal material in our products, and we are proud to be a 100% vegan brand! With the vegan shoe products we produce, we advocate making a production that respects the lives of our animal friends.

Unique Design Guarantee Our talented and young design team knows no rules and goes beyond limits! Each Chekich design gives you the chance to personalize your life as a wearable artwork. It offers you the opportunity to express yourself more with the products you wear and use.

Environment Friendly We value the ecosystem we live in. We handle all our production processes with this sensitivity and create environmentally friendly products.

A Sustainable World We combine fashion and design for a livable world. For the future of the world, we take steps in line with sustainability at every stage of our production.

CHEKICH is Sincere CHEKICH derives its strength from the willingness to touch people's hearts and souls. Thus, it establishes strong ties with individuals who are eager to personalize his appearance and life. CHEKICH, a discreet, global brand, embraces the differences; It is worth interacting with almost anything, including animals and the world we live in. CHEKICH produces things worth sharing. CHEKICH believes that contributing is very valuable. CHEKICH produces 100% vegan products. CHEKICH increases awareness for animals. CHEKICH prefers environmentally friendly production.

CHEKICH Depends on Fair Trade The importance that CHEKICH places on fair trade: It is the product of an institutional culture that values disadvantaged groups and differences, that offers products that its customers can trust in price, quality and after-sales services, that is desired by its business partners to do business, that encourages continuous improvement, that provides a fair and peaceful working environment for employees, that is managed transparently for shareholders, that respects the environment, animals and laws in terms of society.
